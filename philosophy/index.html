With over 25 years of experience in enterprise software development, I work as a freelance Cloud Engineer and DevOps specialist — helping teams design and operate reliable systems on modern cloud infrastructure.

Continuous improvement shapes how I work. Cloud computing, infrastructure automation, and DevOps practices evolve quickly, and keeping pace with relevant developments is what separates solutions that hold up in production from those that become technical debt.

Before designing anything, I invest time in understanding the client’s technical context, business constraints, and the actual problem being solved. Generic architectures rarely fit specific situations well — the goal is always something that creates measurable value, not just something that looks good on paper.

Architecture decisions need to account for the full picture: performance, reliability, operational cost, and long-term maintainability. Cloud platforms and automation tooling help reduce operational overhead and allow systems to scale as requirements evolve — without constantly adding complexity.

Security is part of the design from the start. I follow established practices around least-privilege access, secrets management, and compliance, and integrate them directly into development and deployment pipelines rather than treating them as a final step.

Based in the Ruhrgebiet, I bring reliability and technical thoroughness to every project. I look forward to working with you.
